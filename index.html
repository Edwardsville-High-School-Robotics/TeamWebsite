Welcome to EHS Robotics

We are the Edwardsville High School Robotics Team, competing in FIRST Robotics Competition. Our mission is to inspire young engineers and foster a passion for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

About FIRST Robotics

FIRST Robotics Competition is a global STEM competition where high school students work with mentors and professionals to design, build, and program robots to compete against teams from around the world.

Our Mission

Build innovative robots and develop the next generation of engineers and leaders.
