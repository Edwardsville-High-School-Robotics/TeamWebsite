We are Team 32311 of the Edwardsville High School Robotics program, located in Edwardsville, Illinois. As an active member of FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology), we compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), where we design, build, and program robots to compete against teams from across the nation.

Our team transitioned to the FRC program in the 2025-2026 school year, bringing our experience and passion from the VEX Robotics / BotBall competition. This move marks an exciting new chapter as we tackle more advanced engineering challenges and collaborate with a larger robotics community.
