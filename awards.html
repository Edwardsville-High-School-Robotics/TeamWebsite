Awards & Recognition

The EHS Robotics team has demonstrated exceptional excellence throughout our participation in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FTC). Our commitment to innovation, teamwork, and technical proficiency has earned us numerous prestigious accolades. Below is a comprehensive overview of the significant awards and achievements we have acquired.

Judge's Choice Award

During our inaugural year of competition, the EHS Robotics team proudly received the Judge's Choice Award at the Southern Regional Qualifier Competition held in Illinois. This recognition reflected the judges' appreciation for our team's outstanding engineering design, innovation, and sportsmanship demonstrated throughout the regional competition.
